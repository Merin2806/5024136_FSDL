About Our College

FCRIT, the Fr. Conceicao Rodrigues Institute of Technology, is a premier engineering college located in Bandra, Mumbai, India. Established in 1994, our institution has been at the forefront of technological education and innovation.

At FCRIT, we are committed to providing quality education through a blend of theoretical knowledge and practical experience.
