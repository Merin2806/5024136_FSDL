About Our College

FCRIT, the Fr. Conceicao Rodrigues Institute of Technology, is a premier engineering college located in Bandra, Mumbai, India. Established in 1994, our institution has been at the forefront of technological education and innovation for over three decades. We offer a comprehensive range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering and technology, including Computer Science, Mechanical Engineering, Electronics and Telecommunication, and Information Technology.

At FCRIT, we are committed to providing quality education through a blend of theoretical knowledge and practical experience. Our faculty consists of experienced professors and industry experts who guide students in their academic journey. We boast state-of-the-art facilities including modern laboratories, well-equipped classrooms, a vast library with extensive digital resources, and advanced research centers.

Our campus provides a supportive learning environment with various extracurricular activities, sports facilities, and student clubs. We emphasize holistic development, encouraging students to participate in technical competitions, cultural events, and community service. With a strong focus on research and innovation, FCRIT has produced numerous successful alumni who have made significant contributions in various fields globally.

For more information, visit our official website.
